About

Leonardo Sartori is a pianist and composer from Treviso (IT). His music ranges from piano solo to orchestral composition. In April 2021 he launched his first single "Notturno", written in 2020 in which he expresses his loneliness due to the covid-19 pandemic.
In December 2021 he released "Falling Snow".
He currently focuses on composing neoclassical music.
